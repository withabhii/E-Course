Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us.

Our dedicated support team aims to respond to all email and form inquiries within 1 business day (usually much faster!). For urgent matters during business hours, we recommend calling our support line for the quickest assistance. WhatsApp queries are typically answered within a few hours during business hours.

To help us assist you efficiently, please include:
  • Your full name and the email address associated with your Logic Minds account (if applicable).
  • A clear description of your question or issue.
  • The name of the course you're inquiring about (if relevant).
  • For technical issues, please mention the device (e.g., Laptop, Mobile), operating system (e.g., Windows, iOS), and browser (e.g., Chrome, Safari) you are using.
  • Any relevant screenshots (if possible via email).

Yes, we offer phone support for urgent technical issues during our business hours. However, for many technical problems, submitting a detailed message via email or the contact form allows our technical team to investigate thoroughly and provide a more comprehensive solution. We may ask for screenshots or specific details that are easier to share via email.

Logic Minds operates primarily as an online education platform. While our headquarters address is listed, visits are generally by appointment only. If you have a specific reason to meet in person (e.g., for a partnership discussion), please contact us first via email or phone to schedule an appointment with the relevant department.

Logic Minds is a premier online learning platform committed to providing high-quality, accessible education to help individuals achieve their professional goals.
